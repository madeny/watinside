We are excited to announce version 1.2 of WatInside! This update focuses on performance and user-requested features.

### What's New?

*   **Dark Mode Support**: Full support for iOS system dark mode.
*   **Faster Search**: We've optimized the search algorithm. Finding your winter coat is now instant.
*   **Cloud Sync Improvements**: Syncing across devices is now faster and more reliable.
*   **Export to PDF**: You can now export your box manifests to PDF for printing.

Update now on the App Store!
