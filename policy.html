Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

1. Overview

Watinside is designed to protect your privacy. Your data lives on your device and, if you choose to, it can be synchronized with your private iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit framework. We do not collect, store, or track any personal data on our servers.

2. What Data Is Stored

  • Storage Names, Item Photos, Tags, Notes: Stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud Sync: If iCloud is enabled, your data is synced privately between your devices using your Apple ID via CloudKit.

3. No Third-Party Sharing

Your data is never sent to any third-party servers such as GitHub or Cloudinary.

  • Feedback and bug reports that include optional screenshots are uploaded anonymously using a temporary link to Cloudinary only when you explicitly send feedback.
  • GitHub is used solely to submit feedback and bug reports via issues, but no personal data or app data is ever transmitted automatically.

4. Data Deletion

Deleting the Watinside app from your device does not automatically delete your data from iCloud. However, you can delete all your stored data—including synced iCloud data—by using the “Reset All Data” option from within the app's settings, as long as your iCloud account is still connected.

5. User Control

  • Data stays fully in your control.
  • You can choose to skip iCloud entirely and use Watinside offline.
  • At any time, you can clear all app data via the settings.

6. Contact

If you have questions about your privacy, please contact us at: hello@watinside.com
