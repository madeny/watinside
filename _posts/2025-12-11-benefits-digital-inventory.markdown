In the digital age, we track our steps, our calories, and our finances on our phones. Why not our physical possessions?

## Insurance Claims
If disaster strikes (fire, flood, theft), having a documented inventory with photos is invaluable for insurance claims.

## Duplicate Purchases
Ever bought a pack of batteries only to come home and find you already had two? Knowing what you have saves you money.

## Peace of Mind
There is a certain calmness that comes from knowing exactly where everything is. It reduces the mental load of "stuff management."
