## The "Memory Test" (Why You Will Lose Money)

Insurance adjusters have a saying: *"If it isn't documented, it didn't exist."*

After a fire, flood, or burglary, you are in a state of shock. Asking your brain to recall the brand of your toaster or the model number of your TV is impossible.

Without a detailed list, insurance companies often default to the cheapest replacement value.
* **Without proof:** They pay for a generic "Toaster" ($15).
* **With proof:** They pay for your "Breville Smart Oven Pro" ($280).

That single difference, multiplied across your entire house, can cost you tens of thousands of dollars.
